Summer at Siemens

Overview

For the past summer, I worked as a UX design intern at Siemens Corporate Technology. During the 12 weeks, I worked closely with the designers of UXD department , to make complex feel simple and make experience better. I have accomplished 3 projects:

  1. In the simit software project, I designed interfaces of several functions such as Simulation, Deploy, Vision, Error, Scan and Calibration, created high-fidelity interactive mockups and developed interactive specifications. Due to the strong interest in visual programming, I in-depth researched and designed the If-Then Tool.

  2. I also participated in the research of User-AI collaborative experience, combed the challenges of the experience and designed the journey maps.

  3. Moreover, I visualized and designed infographics for Siemens’ digital enterprise solutions.

Due to NDA, I am unable to present any visuals or elaborate how does the product work. If you’d like to know more about my experience, please feel free to contact me at tarolancy@gmail.com.
